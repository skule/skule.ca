University of Toronto Engineering Society

Founded in 1885, we are the student government for Skule™, the student body at the University of Toronto’s Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering. We provide services, funding, and countless extracurricular opportunities to over 5500 members, proudly continuing the traditions and storied history of Canada’s oldest and largest engineering school.
